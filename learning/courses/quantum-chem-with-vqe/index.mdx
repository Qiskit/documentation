# Overview

This course covers building and mapping a chemical Hamiltonian for evaluation on a quantum computer. This introductory treatment focuses on the variational quantum eigensolver (VQE). Complete the course to earn your IBM Quantum Chemistry with VQE badge.

## Exam

To earn your Quantum Chemistry with VQE badge, take the exam at IBM Training, linked below. This exam is intended to be taken after reading the lessons in this course. After you pass the exam, you will be notified by Credly that you have earned a badge.

[Exam](/learning/courses/quantum-chem-with-vqe/exam)
