# TwoQubitControlledUDecomposer

<Class id="qiskit.synthesis.TwoQubitControlledUDecomposer" isDedicatedPage={true} github="https://github.com/Qiskit/qiskit/tree/main/qiskit/synthesis/two_qubit/two_qubit_decompose.py#L267-L399" signature="qiskit.synthesis.TwoQubitControlledUDecomposer(rxx_equivalent_gate, euler_basis='ZXZ')" modifiers="class">
  Bases: [`object`](https://docs.python.org/3/library/functions.html#object)

  Decompose a general two-qubit unitary in terms of a target two-qubit gate, that is locally equivalent to an [`RXXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate").

  **Synthesis algorithm**

  Any two-qubit unitary $U$ can be written, through its canonical (Weyl) decomposition (see [`TwoQubitWeylDecomposition`](qiskit.synthesis.TwoQubitWeylDecomposition "qiskit.synthesis.TwoQubitWeylDecomposition")), as a Weyl gate $U_d(a, b, c)$ surrounded by four single-qubit unitary gates:

  ```text
       в”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җ
  q_0: в”Ө c2r в”ңв”Ө0      в”ңв”Ө c1r в”ң
       в”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Өв”Ӯ  Weyl в”Ӯв”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Ө
  q_1: в”Ө c2l в”ңв”Ө1      в”ңв”Ө c1l в”ң
       в””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳ
  ```

  The Weyl gate factorizes into a product of three two-qubit rotations, $U_d(a, b, c) = R_{XX}(a)\, R_{YY}(b)\, R_{ZZ}(c)$:

  ```text
       в”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җ
  q_0: в”Ө0        в”ңв”Ө0        в”ңв”Җв– в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ
       в”Ӯ  Rxx(a) в”Ӯв”Ӯ  Ryy(b) в”Ӯ в”ӮZZ(c)
  q_1: в”Ө1        в”ңв”Ө1        в”ңв”Җв– в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җ
       в””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳ
  ```

  The $R_{YY}$ and $R_{ZZ}$ rotations are then mapped onto $R_{XX}$ rotations using single-qubit basis changes. With $R_{YY}(b) = (S^\dagger \otimes S^\dagger)\, R_{XX}(b)\, (S \otimes S)$:

  ```text
       в”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җ
  q_0: в”Ө Sdg в”ңв”Ө0        в”ңв”Ө S в”ң
       в”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Өв”Ӯ  Rxx(b) в”Ӯв”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Ө
  q_1: в”Ө Sdg в”ңв”Ө1        в”ңв”Ө S в”ң
       в””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳ
  ```

  and $R_{ZZ}(c) = (H \otimes H)\, R_{XX}(c)\, (H \otimes H)$:

  ```text
       в”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җ
  q_0: в”Ө H в”ңв”Ө0        в”ңв”Ө H в”ң
       в”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Өв”Ӯ  Rxx(c) в”Ӯв”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Ө
  q_1: в”Ө H в”ңв”Ө1        в”ңв”Ө H в”ң
       в””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳ
  ```

  Finally, each $R_{XX}$ rotation is realized with the user-supplied gate that is locally equivalent to [`RXXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate") (the `rxx_equivalent_gate`), wrapped by the single-qubit gates that account for the local equivalence and for any scaling of the rotation angle. After every rotation is expanded, all single-qubit gates that fall between two consecutive two-qubit gates are multiplied together and consolidated, so the synthesized circuit uses at most three applications of `rxx_equivalent_gate` and at most eight single-qubit unitary gates:

  ```text
       в”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җв”Ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”җ
  q_0: в”Ө d2r в”ңв”Ө0          в”ңв”Ө d1r в”ңв”Ө0          в”ңв”Ө e1r в”ңв”Ө0          в”ңв”Ө f1r в”ң
       в”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Өв”Ӯ  Equiv(a) в”Ӯв”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Өв”Ӯ  Equiv(b) в”Ӯв”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Өв”Ӯ  Equiv(c) в”Ӯв”ңв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Ө
  q_1: в”Ө d2l в”ңв”Ө1          в”ңв”Ө d1l в”ңв”Ө1          в”ңв”Ө e1l в”ңв”Ө1          в”ңв”Ө f1l в”ң
       в””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳв””в”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”Җв”ҳ
  ```

  Here `Equiv(a)`, `Equiv(b)` and `Equiv(c)` are the user-supplied `rxx_equivalent_gate` (the gate locally equivalent to [`RXXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate")) realizing the $R_{XX}(a)$, $R_{XX}(b)$ and $R_{XX}(c)$ rotations, and the remaining boxes are the consolidated single-qubit unitary gates.

  The number of two-qubit gates actually emitted depends on the Weyl parameters of the target: rotations with a vanishing angle are dropped, so unitaries that are closer to a single or two instances of [`RXXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate") use one or two applications of `rxx_equivalent_gate` respectively instead of three. A target close to the identity will use no applications of it.

  **Parameters**

  *   **rxx\_equivalent\_gate** ([*type*](qiskit.dagcircuit.DAGDepNode#type "qiskit.dagcircuit.DAGDepNode.type")*\[*[*Gate*](qiskit.circuit.Gate "qiskit.circuit.Gate")*]*) вҖ“ Gate that is locally equivalent to an [`RXXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate"): $U \sim U_d(\alpha, 0, 0) \sim \text{Ctrl-U}$ gate. Valid options are \[[`RZZGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RZZGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RZZGate"), [`RXXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate"), [`RYYGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RYYGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RYYGate"), [`RZXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RZXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RZXGate"), [`CPhaseGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.CPhaseGate "qiskit.circuit.library.CPhaseGate"), [`CRXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.CRXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.CRXGate"), [`CRYGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.CRYGate "qiskit.circuit.library.CRYGate"), [`CRZGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.CRZGate "qiskit.circuit.library.CRZGate")].
  *   **euler\_basis** ([*str*](https://docs.python.org/3/library/stdtypes.html#str)) вҖ“ Basis string to be provided to [`OneQubitEulerDecomposer`](qiskit.synthesis.OneQubitEulerDecomposer "qiskit.synthesis.OneQubitEulerDecomposer") for 1Q synthesis. Valid options are \[`'ZXZ'`, `'ZYZ'`, `'XYX'`, `'XZX'`, `'U'`, `'U3'`, `'U321'`, `'U1X'`, `'PSX'`, `'ZSX'`, `'ZSXX'`, `'RR'`].

  **Raises**

  [**QiskitError**](exceptions#qiskit.exceptions.QiskitError "qiskit.exceptions.QiskitError") вҖ“ If the gate is not locally equivalent to an [`RXXGate`](qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate "qiskit.circuit.library.RXXGate").
