# Create a Classroom Account

An IBM Quantum&reg; Classroom Account is a simple way for educators to set up and manage access for their students to use IBM Quantum hardware under the Open Plan, without the need to enter credit card information. The Open Plan provides 10 minutes of quantum processing unit (QPU) usage per 28-day rolling window.

## Submit a request

Fill out [this form](https://ibm.biz/classroom-account) to submit a request for a Classroom Account.

## Activate the account

Approved Classroom Account applicants will receive an email (WITHIN SOME TIME FRAME?) that contains a 32-digit feature code. (MENTION THAT THEY SHOULD KEEP THE EMAIL BECAUSE IT HAS INFO ON HOW TO SEND THE ACCOUNT ID BACK TO IBM?) Once you have a feature code, follow the activation steps below:

<Admonition type="caution" title="Do not enter credit card details">
When creating an account with a feature code, do not provide credit card information on IBM Cloud&reg; unless you are actively upgrading to a paid account and are willing to accept being billed for consumed services.
</Admonition>

- Visit https://cloud.ibm.com/.
- Click **Create account**. *Note that you must create a new account even if you already have your own personal account,* because the Classroom Account is not attached to your personal account.
- Enter a valid email address and a password, then click **Next**.
- Check your email inbox for a seven-digit verification code, then return to the account creation form. Enter that code to verify your new account.
- Enter your first and last name, and your country or region. (IS THIS WHERE THE ACCOUNT NAME COMES FROM? SHOULD THEY USE THE SCHOOL'S NAME HERE?)
- Complete the Account notice and click **Continue**.
- Complete the Account privacy notice and click **Continue**.
- At the Verify identity screen, click on the **Register with a code** option. 
- Enter your 32-digit feature code in the **Enter code** text box. Click **Create account** to proceed.
- Navigate to your [account settings on IBM Quantum Platform](https://quantum.cloud.ibm.com/account) and copy the Account ID (found under Details). Send this ID number to the IBM Quantum team, and they will process the Classroom Account for full activation.
